FAMILY UNITY CELEBRATION PLANNED IN BAMENDJOU

August 13 at 12:30 PM

BAMENDJOU, Cameroon

august is the time at which bamedjou becomes the place to be

Fotsing Emmanuel

a large family gathering is scheduled to take place on 13 August 2026 in the village of Bamendjou in the West Region of Cameroon. The event is expected to bring together family members from different places to strengthen unity and discuss important family matters.

according to the organizing committee, the meeting will provide an opportunity for relatives to reconnect, share ideas, and reinforce the bonds that hold the family together. Community members and invited guests are also expected to attend the occasion.

the gathering will not only focus on discussions but will also include moments of celebration. Participants will enjoy a variety of traditional meals, music, and cultural dancing, creating an atmosphere of joy and togetherness.
